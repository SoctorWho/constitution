\subsection{The aim of the society}
\begin{enumerate}[i.]
    \item To provide opportunities for Doctor Who fans (also known as Whovians) to interact with each other and discuss ideas.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{The objectives of the society}
\begin{enumerate}[i.]
    \item To provide a platform to allow students to socialise, discuss episodes \& stories of Doctor Who.
    \item To provide events such as watch-alongs, quizzes or similar.
    \item To promote attendance of other Doctor Who related events.
\end{enumerate}
